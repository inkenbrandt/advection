Energy Balance Closure
======================

This guide explains the concepts behind energy balance closure and how this package helps to address it.

Why closure matters
-------------------

Eddy-covariance towers routinely **under-sum the available
energy**—the measured sensible (H) plus latent (LE) heat flux is
typically 10–20 % lower than :math:`R_n - G`.
